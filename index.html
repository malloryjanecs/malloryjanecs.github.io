About me

Background

In 2011, I left University to pursue my career as a draftsman and maintained that job for several years. In 2015 I had the wonderful opportunity to move abroad, so I resigned from my job and moved to Germany. While in Germany, I spent my time exploring other cultures and delicious food from countries such as Italy, Hungary, Austria, Ireland, Malta, and even South Africa. During this time, I also made the decision to return to higher education as a non-traditional student and make a career change to a subject I was passionate about: technology. I enrolled in college with the major of "Computer Science" in 2018.

Present Day

I now have an Associate's in Computer Studies and am approximately 20 credits from graduating with a BSc. Now that I am back in the United States, I am excited to return to the workforce and expand my skill set in order to be a vital team member within a company.

Goals

Since returning to higher education after a long hiatus, I maintain several academic goals such as graduating Summa Cum Laude and finishing a Bachelor in Computer Science ahead of my original anticipated timeline. Additionally, I intend to continue independent learning upon graduation in order to remain informed on the latest technology.
